Improved Coke Oven
Electrifying old tech
<&advanced_coke_oven>
The Improved Coke Oven does the same thing as the basic Coke Oven, but faster, with I/O, and can be sped up with power.
With coal or wood being inputed in the top, creosote comes out the bottom, charcoal or coke out the front, and power in the back.
